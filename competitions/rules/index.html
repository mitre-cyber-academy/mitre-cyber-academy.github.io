Competition Rules

Guidelines put in place to provide a fair and fun competition

Rules and etiquette for the MITRE Capture the Flag

Rules

  • Absolutely no flag sharing! (Why help your competition?)
  • You may not do anything to diminish the competition experience for other players on the system; if you are worried that you might break something, ask an operator in Discord (with a private message) for clarification. We love creativity, just clear it with us first.
  • No automated tool usage (nmap, metasploit, nessus, etc.) is allowed against our infrastructure. If you feel a challenge requires the use of one of these tools, please ask an operator to verify.
  • Foul play will not be tolerated (first offense is a warning, second offense is a 10-50 point deduction and third offense is a disqualification)
  • No bullying. This includes harassment, intimidation and threats.
  • Administrator/White cell decisions are final

Etiquette

  • Keep it clean in Discord and MPAA G-rated conversations, please
  • Remember that Administrators/White Cell in Discord channel are running the game
  • Be a good sport throughout the competition

Flag Formats

All flags in the competition will match the following format unless otherwise specified on the challenge description: MCA{flag} such as MCA{ch@llen3e_flag-Is H3r3}
